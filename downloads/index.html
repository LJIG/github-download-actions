Book Review Archives

Book reviews provide concise analyses of new books from the Military Review library. These are not simply book summaries. Reviewers provide their educated opinions on authors’ writing style, sources, comprehensiveness, and supporting illustrations.

Below you will find our online archives going back to January 2017. Prior to 2017, book reviews were published in every edition of Military Review. Those issues can be found in the English Edition Archives.
